What is MyEyesOnly?

Think of OnlyFans but for collectible photos/videos. Your fans can bid or purchase a photo or video as a unique collectible in the form of an NFT crypto currency. This means your fans can purchase your photos and will be the owner of that content.
You get paid in Ethereum for the initial purchase, and collect royalties every time your photo is sold again.

What is not MyEyesOnly?

Unlike OnlyFans where fans can only see photos, MyEyesOnly is a marketplace where you can sell individual photos to individual people. Once you sell that photo you can never sell it again (unless you buy it back). MyEyesOnly lets you sell unique photos for your fans to collect, trade, and resell.
You get paid for the initial sale and you collect royalties every time the photo is resold.
